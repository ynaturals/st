1. Xylan® Coatings are a family of fluoropolymer coatings designed for use on various types of OEM components and fasteners. Most Xylan® coatings contain PTFE or other types of lubricants and are applied in thin films.



  1. Xylan Coatings provides:

  2. Lubrication and controlled friction

  3. Wear resistance

  4. Heat resistance

  5. Nonstick and release properties

  6. Can also protect from corrosion



Coating application in:

  1. For offshore applications bolts

  2. Nuts

  3. Washers

  4. Clamps

  5. Desalination plant parts

  6. Flats

  7. M.S. angles

  8. M.S. channels

  9. For bolts

  10. Nuts

  11. Studs

  12. Washers

  13. M.S.structures like channels, angles, roof top surfaces for atmospheric corrosion protection

  14. M.S.clamps

  15. Storage containers

  16. Pistons

  17. Dry bushes

  18. Dry lubrication applications

  19. Actuators

  20. Cylinders
